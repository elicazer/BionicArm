Servo Connections (Each servo):
===============================
Red Wire    → PCA9685 V+ (power)
Brown/Black → PCA9685 GND (ground)
Orange/Yellow → PCA9685 Channel (signal)

Channel Assignments:
===================
Channel 0 → Thumb Servo
Channel 1 → Index Servo
Channel 2 → Middle Servo
Channel 3 → Ring Servo
Channel 4 → Pinky Servo

Power Requirements:
==================
Arduino: USB power from phone (via OTG cable)
Servos: External 5V 3-5A power supply
Common Ground: Connect all GND together

I2C Connections:
===============
Arduino A4 → PCA9685 SDA
Arduino A5 → PCA9685 SCL
Arduino 5V → PCA9685 VCC
Arduino GND → PCA9685 GND

Safety Notes:
============
- Always connect common ground
- Use adequate power supply for servos
- Double-check wiring before powering on
- Test individual servos before full assembly
